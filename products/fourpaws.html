What it is

Four Paws Training & Enrichment Academy™ is a working demonstration of how the 4P3X base architecture can power a full learning management system — adapted for the pet training and canine behaviour education sector, with course delivery, lesson progression, quizzes, certificates, and a trainer command centre.

Where it can be used

Dog trainers, canine behaviour coaches, pet education brands, puppy classes, online training academies, and enrichment businesses looking to deliver structured digital courses.

Why it was built

Many pet training businesses deliver value through knowledge and programmes but lack the digital tools to scale — no structured LMS, no certificate system, no AI-assisted client coaching. This demonstrates the gap and the solution.

When it is useful

When a trainer wants to move clients from in-person-only to a blended or fully digital learning experience — with structured courses, progress tracking, and automated certificate delivery.

Who it helps

Dog trainers, canine behaviour specialists, enrichment coaches, pet business owners, and online training course creators.

How it works

Features a Trainer Intelligence OS for managing clients, deploying AI coaching assistants, tracking progress, and administering courses — plus a client-facing PWA for course content, lesson completion, quiz-gated progression, and certificate download.

Architecture behind it

Refactored from the 4P3X base with education-sector LMS logic, trainer/client split, quiz and certificate data model, and AI coaching integration points.

Demo Mode to Live Mode pathway

Demo Mode shows the full LMS experience with sample courses and client data. Live Mode connects real customer accounts, payment gateways, content management, certificate issuing, and trainer dashboards.

What it can become next

Could expand to include video lesson delivery, live virtual classes, NFC/QR client onboarding, franchise trainer networks, and marketplace distribution for training content.

Investor and employer relevance

Demonstrates the creator's range — from enterprise fleet management to consumer pet education — proving the architecture can serve radically different markets with the same underlying codebase.
